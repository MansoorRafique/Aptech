ABOUT HINDU RELIGION
Hindu Religion

One of the major festivals of India, Holi is celebrated with enthusiasm and gaiety on the full moon day in the month of Phalgun which is the month of March as per the Gregorian calendar.

Hindu Events

Holi:

Holi is celebrated at the approach of vernal equinox, on the Phalguna Purnima and typically comes in March, sometimes February in the Gregorian Calendar

Diwali:

Diwali or Divali also known as Deepavali and the "festival of lights", is an ancient Hindu festival celebrated in autumn every year.The festival spiritually signifies the victory of light over darkness.

Raksha-Bandhan:

Raksha Bandhan is Hindu festival that celebrates the love and duty between brothers and sisters; the festival is also popularly used to celebrate any brother-sister b/w men and women who are relatives.

Dashera:

The first nine days are celebrated as Maha Navratri or Sharada Navratri and culminates on the tenth day as Dussehra.ijayadashami also known as Dussehra..
